Hunger at Home

I joined Hunger at Home in 2018 and then together with Ewell Sterner, CEO of Hunger at Home, founded the Youth Council in 2020 during the midst of the pandemic. Since 2021, I have become the Vice President of the Hunger at Home Youth Council. Hunger at Home Youth Council is made up of students with a passion for ending food insecurity and growing sustainability efforts in our community

Activities

As part of the Youth Council, we provide volunteers for the weekly food distributions as well as hosting webinars and canned food drives. as a youth council, we have been able to reach ovher hundreds of students from various different schools as well as two colleges which we work closly with.

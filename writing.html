Saratoga Falcon and Soundings

I have been a part of my school's newspaper, the Saratoga Falcon and editor for my school's literary and art magazine, Soundings, for the past four and two years respectively. I started out in the newspaper being a reporter for two years and then the Entertainment Editor and now Opinion Editor.

It has always been fun for me to have been able to cover local events around my school as well as provide students a place to present their work in Soundings. We publish around nine newspapers and two Soundings mazagines every school year.
